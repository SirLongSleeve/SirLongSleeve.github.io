About Me

I'm a Daphne Jackson Fellow at the University of Nottingham within the Advanced Materials Research Group in the Faculty of Engineering, I specialize in the acceleration of molecular simulations through the application of advanced machine learning methodologies. My research focus has centered on addressing this challenge in the context of smaller scale systems, leveraging Gaussian Processes throughout the course of my doctoral studies. Presently, as part of my postdoctoral research, I am extending my inquiry to encompass bulk surface materials, employing high-dimensional neural networks to further enhance the depth and sophistication of my research.
